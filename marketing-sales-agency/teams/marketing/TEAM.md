The Marketing division owns the top and middle of the funnel — awareness, education, and consideration. Led by the CMO, it runs four parallel lanes: **research** (intel, SEO audits, analytics), **content** (blogs, email, landing pages), **community** (Reddit, X, HN, LinkedIn organic), and **paid media** (Google, Meta, LinkedIn ads). The Research Analyst is the shared intelligence layer — all three other specialists pull signals from it.
